## Criteria Notes

- Each image teaches one concept and fits the selected purpose.
- The images match nearby Markdown and do not replace Markdown instructions.
- No in-image text, labels, Chinese characters, citations, safety warnings, correctness badges, brand marks, identifiable faces, red pain marks, clinical framing, combat framing, weapons, targets, or opponents were observed.
- The muscle-attention image uses broad blue regions only and avoids exact anatomy labels, exposed musculature, and pain/injury marks.
- Color is not the only teaching channel because nearby Markdown names the regions and explains the cues.

## Disposition

Review result: approved for M3 promotion.

Static images cannot prove exact Baduanjin form quality, balance safety, or reader understanding.
Beginner comprehension proof and text-only rollback proof remain downstream M4 obligations.
