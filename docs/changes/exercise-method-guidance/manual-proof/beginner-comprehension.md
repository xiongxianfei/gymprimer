# M4 Beginner Comprehension Proof

## Scope

- Change ID: `exercise-method-guidance`
- Milestone: M4
- Proof ID: EMG-M2
- Pages checked: the six proof-slice exercise pages from R28 and R29, plus promoted pattern previews that mention those exercises.
- Criteria: a beginner reader can identify the starting point, effort, stop condition, and that the page is not a personal program.
- Check date: 2026-07-04
- Reader profile: non-identifying beginner-reader proxy using a plain-language checklist; no private health details, training logs, names, contact details, or symptoms were collected.

## Result

Pass for M4 implementation handoff.

Each proof-slice page has a visible `How much to do` section that directly answers the amount question, names the effort target, gives a stop condition, and links to the shared method principle page for terms such as sets, reps, holds, rest, and progression. The pages read as static examples and do not ask the reader for personal data.
