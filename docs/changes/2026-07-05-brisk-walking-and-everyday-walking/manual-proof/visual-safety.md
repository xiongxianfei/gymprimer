## Review Criteria

The movement image must show:

- upright posture;
- forward gaze;
- relaxed neck and shoulders;
- natural arm swing;
- relaxed hands;
- heel-to-toe walking.

The movement image must avoid:

- no in-image labels;
- no red pain marks;
- no race-walking;
- no running;
- no treadmill;
- no hiking;
- no wearable;
- no wrong/correct framing;
- no clinical framing.

The muscle-attention image must show broad attention regions for:

- glutes;
- thighs;
- calves;
- trunk;
- shoulders and upper back;
- feet and ankles.

The muscle-attention image must avoid:

- no precise anatomy;
- no exposed musculature;
- no in-image labels;
- no diagnosis, treatment, or rehabilitation framing;
- no pain marks;
- no exact activation claims.
