## Criteria Notes

- Each image teaches one distinct concept and fits the selected purpose.
- The images match nearby Markdown and do not replace Markdown instructions.
- No in-image text, labels, badges, citations, warnings, brand marks, identifiable faces, medical framing, red pain marks, or correct/wrong framing were observed.
- The overstride comparison uses neutral overstride framing: no labels, no badges, no red marks, no shaming, and no correct/wrong symbols.
- The muscle-attention image uses broad muscle highlighting only and avoids exact anatomy labels, exposed musculature, and pain or injury marks.
- Color is not the only teaching channel because nearby Markdown names the broad regions and explains the cues.

## Disposition

Review result: approved for M3 promotion.

## Residual Risk

Static images cannot prove running form quality, individualized fit, or reader comprehension.
Beginner comprehension proof remains a downstream M4 obligation.
